Sustainable • Local • Innovative

Circular insulation panels from sheep wool waste — built for energy-efficient modular construction

CYSIP collects and transforms local sheep wool waste into certified structural insulated panels (SIPs) for low-energy buildings. We close the loop between agriculture and construction by turning waste into high-performance, natural insulation.

About CYSIP

CYSIP is dedicated to sustainable innovation in building materials. We combine agricultural by-products, scientific testing, and modern manufacturing to produce certified insulation panels suitable for energy-efficient and modular construction.

Projects & Activities

Research & Testing

Material formulation, thermal performance testing, fire & moisture resistance trials and certification support.

Pilot Production

Small-scale production of SIP prototypes for modular units and field validation.

Supply Chain

Local wool collection with farmers and logistic solutions for scalable supply.
